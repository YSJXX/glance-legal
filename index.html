2. 제3자 제공 및 처리위탁

서비스 운영을 위해 아래 업체에 처리를 위탁하거나 정보가 전달됩니다. 각 업체는 자체 개인정보 처리방침을 따릅니다.

  • Google (OAuth 로그인 / YouTube Data API) — 로그인 인증 및 구독 채널·영상 정보 조회
  • Google Gemini API — 영상 자막 텍스트를 전송하여 요약문 생성
  • Supabase — 데이터베이스 및 인증 호스팅
  • Upstash (Redis) — 작업 큐 및 일일 사용량 관리 (개인정보 장기 저장 없음)
  • Expo — 푸시 알림 발송 인프라

서비스는 광고 네트워크를 사용하지 않으며, 제3자 분석·추적 SDK(예: 애널리틱스 툴)를 포함하지 않습니다.

3. 보관 기간 및 삭제

계정 삭제를 요청하면 유예 기간(grace period) 후 다음 데이터가 영구 삭제됩니다: 저장한 영상, 평가, 푸시 토큰, 채널 구독 목록, OAuth 토큰. 계정 자체는 참조 무결성을 위해 이메일·표시 이름을 익명화한 뒤 보관되며, 로그인 계정(Supabase Auth)은 함께 삭제됩니다.

Privacy Policy

Pith · Effective: July 16, 2026

"Pith" ("the Service") is a personal-use application that automatically summarizes new videos from the YouTube channels a user subscribes to. The Service collects the minimum information necessary to operate and does not use advertising or third-party analytics/tracking.

1. Information We Collect

DataPurpose
Google account email, display nameLogin and account identification
Google OAuth access/refresh tokensAuthenticating calls to the YouTube Data API (read-only scope) on the user's behalf
YouTube subscription list and channels the user chooses to manageBuilding the feed, detecting new videos
Video caption/transcript textGenerating AI summaries (sent to Google Gemini API)
Saved videos, ratings, read/unread statusUsage history and feed personalization
Expo push tokenDelivering new-summary notifications

2. Third-Party Processors

The Service relies on the following processors, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google (OAuth login / YouTube Data API) — authentication and access to subscription/video data
  • Google Gemini API — receives transcript text to generate summaries
  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting
  • Upstash (Redis) — job queue and daily quota tracking (no long-term personal data storage)
  • Expo — push notification delivery infrastructure

The Service does not use advertising networks or third-party analytics/tracking SDKs.

3. Retention and Deletion

When a user requests account deletion, after a grace period the following data is permanently deleted: saved videos, ratings, push tokens, channel subscriptions, and OAuth tokens. The account row itself is retained with email and display name anonymized (for referential integrity), and the underlying auth account is deleted.

4. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data using the in-app account deletion feature or by contacting the email below.

5. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 14 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
